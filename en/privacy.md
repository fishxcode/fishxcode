# FishXCode Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: February 11, 2026**

FishXCode is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with applicable laws including China's Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL), Cybersecurity Law, and Data Security Law.

## 1. What We Collect and Why

| Context | Data Collected | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Account registration | Phone/email, password, username | Account creation, identity verification |
| API usage | Call timestamps, request paths, token consumption | Billing, fraud prevention |
| Device & network | IP address, device ID, browser type, OS | Service security and stability |
| Payments | Handled by Alipay / WeChat Pay | Transaction processing; we do not store full card details |
| Customer support | Contact info, communication records | Identity verification, issue resolution |

::: tip About Model Training
Unless you explicitly enable optional features such as "History" or "Debug Mode", FishXCode does **NOT** use your inputs or outputs to train AI models.
:::

## 2. How We Share Your Information

- **Third-party model providers**: Your input must be transmitted to the model provider you select to fulfill your API request. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.
- **Service partners**: Cloud providers and analytics partners operate under strict data processing agreements.
- **Legal requirements**: We may disclose information when required by law or government authorities.
- We **never sell** your personal information or make unauthorized public disclosures.

## 3. Cross-Border Data Transfers

FishXCode operates within China, and personal data is stored domestically. Any cross-border transfers strictly follow PIPL requirements and obtain your separate consent where required.

## 4. Data Retention & Security

- **Retention**: Data is kept only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose or as required by law; then deleted or anonymized.
- **Security**: We use encryption, de-identification, access controls, and conduct regular security audits.
